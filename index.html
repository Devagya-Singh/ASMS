About DASS

DASS, the Digital Article Submission System, provides a comprehensive and user-friendly platform to manage research articles, publications, and conference submissions. It offers a streamlined process for authors, reviewers, and event organizers.

Key Features:

  • Effortless Article Submission
  • Collaborative Review and Feedback
  • Customizable Conference Pages
  • Real-Time Progress Tracking
  • Advanced Reporting Tools
